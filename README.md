# E-Commerce-Sales-Performance-Report

🧾 Overview

This report analyzes e-commerce sales performance using two datasets: Orders and Details. The objective is to derive actionable business insights related to revenue, profitability, customer behavior, and regional performance.

📌 Key Business Metrics

👉 The business is profitable with a profit margin of ~8.4%, indicating moderate efficiency with room for optimization.

📈 Key Business Insights


1. Revenue Drivers
      Electronics and Clothing together contribute over 70% of total revenue.
      Product strategy should prioritize these categories.
2. Profitability Focus
      Clothing yields strong margins → potential area for scaling.
      Furniture may require cost optimization or pricing adjustments.
3. Geographic Opportunities
      Focus expansion in:
      Maharashtra
      Madhya Pradesh
Investigate losses in Rajasthan (logistics, pricing, or demand issues).
4. Customer Behavior
      High reliance on COD suggests:
      Trust barriers in online payments
      Opportunity to incentivize prepaid orders (discounts, cashback)



🚀 Recommendations


Strategic Actions:

✅ Increase inventory and marketing for Electronics & Clothing

⚠️ Review loss-making regions (Rajasthan)

💡 Introduce digital payment incentives (UPI discounts, cashback)

📦 Optimize Furniture pricing or supply chain costs

🎯 Target high-performing states with localized campaigns



🛠️ Conclusion


The business demonstrates steady growth and profitability, with strong performance in key categories and regions. By addressing underperforming areas and optimizing payment and product strategies, the company can significantly improve margins and scale efficiently.
